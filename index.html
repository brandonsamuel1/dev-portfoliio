#about-me

An ex salesman who become interested in code when I learned how it can automate some of my most tedious tasks. To be honest, my interest in programming probably came before that when I watched iRobot for the very first time. Little did I know the impact that movie had on myself and possibly the world... Anyways, at some point, I really wanted to learn how to code. So what did I do? I reached out to a long time friend, who has been a developer for a few years and asked him how he did it, hows he's enjoying it, what path did he take etc. The next day, yes it was over a few too many drinks, I remember thinking to myself dang, that actually sounds like a really fun environment to be in. So I start to do a little bit of research, how to get started, where to go for resources so on and so fourth, can this actually turn into a great, fulfilling career. Landed on Codecademy and started for free to learn basic HTML/CSS. What stuck out to me the most was, overall, how I felt doing this. That realization of finding what could end up being a strong passion I didn't know I could feel, was all the answers I needed. I told myself, "I want to be great at this". Months later, I conviced my parents I want to do this as a career, and enrolled in Lighthouse Labs where my journey offically got started.
